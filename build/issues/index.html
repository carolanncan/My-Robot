I've listed a few known issues in the site and how I've tried to resolve them. I've also described areas I plan to continue learning.

Stylesheets

You'll notice that all of my media queries are in the "all" stylesheet, but I also have a "mobile" stylesheet. My goal was to keep all of the queries in a separate stylesheet to keep the "all" stylesheet from becoming overwhelming to use.

I had a few strange issues importing my stylesheet and was hitting a wall in my troubleshooting. I've managed to resolve a few of the issues, but would love a chance to look at some of my past commits on GitHub to better understand how to address the issue.

Middleman Features

After I experimented with a few of Middleman's features I became more excited with its features. It definitely expedited the process for getting this site up and running. In the future, I plan to really maximize layouts and little features such as a quick way to make a current state in the nav to indicate which page you are viewing.

Contact Form

Since Middleman is a static site generator, it doesn't have anything readliy available for adding a contact form. I've just utilized Simple Form for now. I decided I'd like to focus my time before the site visit on learning a few additional things, like VIM and HAML. So afterward, I plan on learning a little Ruby to get a good contact form going.

Effectivenes

Overall, I'd really like to review the "all" stylesheet, and think about ways to make it more efficient. I'd also love to receive feedback on how I could better utilize Neat in my layouts. After having built this site, I feel I could better utilize the grid and make my layouts more efficient.
